What is CargoWise One?

CargoWise One is a leading cloud-based transport management system (TMS) used by over 80% of the top 50 global 3PLs. It’s a single-platform solution designed by the WiseTech team, formerly known as ediEnterprise Logistics, to streamline logistics operations for local supply chain providers, global freight forwarders, and everything in between. 

The gigantic ERP eliminates the need for multiple software and databases by providing a single database that can be accessed by multiple users, functions, countries, offices, and languages. For instance, when a purchase order (PO) is entered in CW1 or received via EDI from an operator, the system automatically notifies the required personnel. Everyone can access the same data at the same time. 

With CargoWise One, you can manage freight, integrate with other systems, and consolidate data across your entire organization. It provides specialized tools for various aspects of logistics, including global forwarding, trade compliance, warehouse management, parcel delivery, and supply chain optimization.
